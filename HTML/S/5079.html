 437     # Sleeping Gryphons will counter if attacked, then wake up and
 438     # morph to new Gryphons (a different unit type). The new unit will
 439     # then usually fly away because the AI thinks your attacking unit
 440     # is too tough for it to take.  This could be fixed with some
 441     # WML contortions, but why do it?  The nonstandard behavior adds
 442     # variety and a bit of mystery.  Gryphon Mountain is a magical place.
 443 
 444     #
 445     # If the sleeping gryphon is attacked, it wakes up
 446     #
