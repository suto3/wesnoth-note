   3 # Get a couple quick lower-level units first to start taking
   4 # gold. Also get some level one cannon fodder to help soak up the
   5 # awesome damage capability of the first wave of monsters. Then,
   6 # recall your most powerful units. You need firepower. You also could
   7 # really use the slow ability of netcasters and entanglers. Two
   8 # healers are probably a minimum. Slide the first units around the
   9 # edges getting gold, because your income will be very negative in a
  10 # hurry, and you probably need all the firepower you can get. Don't
  11 # worry about gold too much though--you probably can't start the next
  12 # scenario with more than the minimum. Keshan can handle the upper
  13 # middle section himself. He is good against the tentacles, and even
  14 # the cuttlefish if he stays healed. Put everyone else in the
  15 # middle. Use your auras as much as possible: leadership and
  16 # illuminate (Cylanna *is* a diviner by now isn't she?), and use
  17 # "slows" to keep all your units from dying. You will probably lose a
  18 # couple leveled-up ones anyway.
  19 
  20 # The loyal cuttle fish isn't terribly useful, but cool to have
  21 # anyway. It has to be a Cuttle Fish2 to work around a problem with
  22 # the [advancefrom] tag. When he levels into a kraken, he is quite a
  23 # bit nicer to have. (And pretty cool looking if I do say so myself.)
