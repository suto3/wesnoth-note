   3 # Geographical assumptions:
   4 #
   5 # This campaign was apparently designed before the geography of Wesnoth
   6 # was fixed to the main map -- there was a reference in it to unknown forest
   7 # south of the "Great River", which doesn't fit at all. To rescue this, the
   8 # original tSG Great River has to be either the unnamed river running through
   9 # the Aethenwood or off the map entirely. I've opted for the latter, thus the
  10 # reference to Kerlath being "in the furthermost south", and renamed the river
  11 # as the "Black River". Since the original campaign placed the "elvish woods"
  12 # west of Westin, I'm supposing they're in fact a southern (off-map) lobe
  13 # of the Aethenwood.
  14 #
