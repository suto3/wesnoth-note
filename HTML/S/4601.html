   3 # You're welcome for the free, loyal brawler. There are two cages on the
   4 # shore. One contains Keshan, a drake burner who can be *very* useful
   5 # during the campaign, and the other contains a poacher named
   6 # Siddry. He's not going to be much help here; you will have to work
   7 # to keep him alive, and he won't come with you. However, if you
   8 # succeed, he comes back in The Flaming Sword with some friends who
   9 # are a big help taking villages in the beginning (and not much help
  10 # thereafter). If Siddry lives, he will be stored in a variable until
  11 # then. The shipwreck in the north contains a treasure chest with 100
  12 # gold.
  13 #
  14 # Send someone north to get gold. Recruit about two castles, one of
  15 # which should be all level 2's. Hopefully that includes at least one
  16 # more healer. Healers are *essential* here. Any units close to
  17 # leveling are especially good. Send everyone east. Be aggressive in
  18 # taking out the troops and getting to the leader, but don't leave any
  19 # units out and exposed. You should probably instigate the slave
  20 # rebellion as night comes at the end of your first offensive. That
  21 # will keep some the retalliation against you low, and slow down the
  22 # saurians coming towards you from the north. When it's safe, rescue
  23 # the drake, and try to get him some experience--carefully. After
  24 # taking out the leader, you can recruit a unit or two as
  25 # replacements, but you shouldn't need more than that. You should just
  26 # be getting involved with the first wave from the north leader
  27 # now. (He's slowed down by the water.) Sit tight around the castle
  28 # until the enemy onslaught has thinned out. After that it should be
  29 # no problem wiping out the other two leaders--if you have survived
  30 # this far, you have quite a few high-level units.
