   3 # Hopefully Kai Krellis is at least half leveled up
   4 # already. Otherwise, this can be painful. Recruit a buch of citizens
   5 # and brawlers, and send them east with Krellis. Use citizens to lure
   6 # the skeleton archer types into the water. They are easy pickings
   7 # there for other citizens, brawlers, and Krellis. One of those guys
   8 # gets Krellis half way up to level one. A few citizens should also
   9 # survive to become brawlers. About the time you get Krellis leveled
  10 # up, and you are thinking about retreating everyone else, the bats
  11 # will arrive. Use hunters and initiates to clear a path back
  12 # west. Send three or four expendable units with Krellis. Their job is
  13 # only to keep him from getting stuck in a ZOC. Krellis runs for the
  14 # corner, while the rest of the troops gain experience doing a
  15 # fighting retreat from the skeletons, and other bad guys arriving in
  16 # the middle of the map. Ignore gold for now. You don't need much on
  17 # the next level.
  18 
  19 # Killing all the enemy leaders will end the level, but that won't be
  20 # easy. Killing any enemy is good though. Each one gives you a
  21 # different bonus: Cash, a loyal bat, or a ring of strength.
  22 
  23 # If one of the named enemies is killed, a variable is stored. That
  24 # way, the same names won't get used in the last level.
