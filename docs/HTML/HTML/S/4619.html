   3 # This level is not too hard. Don't go too negative on gold, but
   4 # recruit three or four high-level units. You need mostly leveled
   5 # initiates for their magic ranged attacks. Keshan is a big help, and
   6 # so is the unit with the storm trident. If you decide to go after the
   7 # orc leader, wait until the undead are dispatched. Heal everyone,
   8 # then assemble just to the north of his keep. Don't touch the ships
   9 # or piers though! Wait until day time, then send in all the units
  10 # with magical attacks. Use other units to keep the slurbows
  11 # busy. Hopefully the leader won't kill anyone before your next
  12 # turn. One more round of magical attacks should kill him before his
  13 # recruits start beating you up. Killing the orc leader isn't really
  14 # necessary, but he helps someone level up, and he has a fair bit of
  15 # gold. The next level is much easier with lots of gold.
  16 
  17 # A lot of the code here is to activate the orc side if the mermen do
  18 # anything wrong, and keep it passive until then. There is also some
  19 # animation of the orcs coming out to tear down the keep after the
  20 # first turn.
