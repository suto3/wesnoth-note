   1 \begin{abstract}
   2 
   3 Late 2007 I started to rewrite the gui engine of Wesnoth, this project is called
   4 the gui2 project. A lot of code and documentation has been written since. The
   5 documentation is available at:
   6 \begin{itemize}
   7 \item \url{http://wiki.wesnoth.org/GUIToolkit} The general information
   8         regarding the project and documentation regarding the WML used.
   9 \item \url{http://devdocs.wesnoth.org} The general doxygen information.
  10 \item \url{http://devdocs.wesnoth.org/pages.html} Contains some links to more
  11         gui2 specific information.
  12 \end{itemize}
  13 
  14 This documentation describes a lot of the details and the wiki describes some of
  15 the design from the WML side. Doxygen contains some information regarding the
  16 design of the C++ side but it misses the overall design. This paper tries to
  17 fill that gap, trying to explain more of the overall design and some of the
  18 design choices.
  19 
  20 \end{abstract}
