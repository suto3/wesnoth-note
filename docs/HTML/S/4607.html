   4 # can do lots of damage to the other. Here's where you can use all the
   5 # piercing units you have. Hopefully you haven't been totally ignoring
   6 # the spearman and warrior lines. Even level one units can do pretty
   7 # high damage to drakes though, so you can level up some more
   8 # here. Don't get any units besides piercing ones. Netcasters aren't
   9 # really necessary, and one diviner is enough. Actually, don't get
  10 # *any* units for about four turns. The drakes will take a while
  11 # getting to you, so just take some villages and let your income
  12 # work. Don't worry about *holding* the villages, you'll get a bonus
  13 # in the end if you survive. Just worry about getting enough units
  14 # that you do survive. Let the first wave of drakes come to
  15 # you. You'll have the advantage in the water--even against high-level
  16 # units. They will kill some of your weaker units, but you will do far
  17 # more damage to them, and level up some new ones in the process. If
  18 # you survive the first wave, you should be fine. You will have to do
  19 # a little fighting on land, but you can stay mostly in villages at
  20 # least. Just be careful about how many units can attack each of
  21 # yours.
