   3 # The Drake phoneme inventory a writing system must cover is as follows:
   4 #
   5 # Vowels:     /a/ /au/ /e/ /i/ /o/ /u/

   7 #             /th/ /sh/ /ch/ /'/

  10 # English and many other Indo-European languages are /f/ /h/ /j/ /p/ /w/ /y/
  11 # /z/.

  13 # Final 'hn' is a nasalization marker.  Final 'é' indicates the vowel
  14 # is spoken rather than modifying a medial vowel (there is no silent
  15 # final 'e' in the language). Word-final 'ck' has the value /k/.
